Our Story

Tenzin Norbu, originally from Thimphu, Bhutan, and Sanya Yeh, a New York native raised in Lancaster, PA, crossed paths while working together in New York City's diamond district, specializing in B2B sales. After tying the knot, they made the decision to establish their family roots in Pennsylvania. At that time, there was a noticeable absence of Bhutanese restaurants in the area, and they were passionate about introducing the unique and delectable flavors of Bhutan and Nepal to the local community.


In 2016, they embarked on their culinary journey by opening their first restaurant, Yak n Yeti, in New Cumberland. The overwhelming love and support from the community encouraged them to expand their venture, leading to the launch of Yak n Yeti II in Carlisle, PA, in 2018. Their dream of becoming a part of Lancaster finally came to fruition in 2021 when they opened Norbu.


Though the closure of their New Cumberland location was a bittersweet moment due to staffing challenges, Tenzin and Sanya are eager to embark on this new chapter with Norbu. They are committed to serving and connecting with their patrons, all while actively supporting the local community, which they believe is the lifeblood of our neighborhoods. Join them in this exciting culinary adventure as they continue to share their love for Bhutanese and Nepali cuisine with you!
